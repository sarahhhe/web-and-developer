ABOUT US

Hi! We’re a husband and wife team making clean, simple websites for small businesses. Sarah does all the design and coding by hand — HTML, CSS, and a love for layout. Karem takes care of communication, planning, and making sure everything runs smooth from start to finish.We started this because we believe good websites don’t need to be complicated, expensive, or filled with fluff. Just thoughtful design, clear communication, and code that actually works. When you work with us, you’ll always know who you’re talking to, and exactly who is building your site (Sarah). We’re friendly, organised and completely transparent. If you’re looking for a stress-free experience and a site that feels just right, we’d love to work with you.
